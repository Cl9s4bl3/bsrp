Who are we

We've been playing Bricks Rigs for over 2 years now, and we always wanted to revolutionize the roleplaying experience. In 2022 we have decided to lauch our own Bricks Rigs server, which has led to the point, where we can proudly say, that we've achived our beloved goal: Becoming one of the best roleplaying server and community. We've started expanding our server fleet by starting to develop a Roblox version of BSRP to be able to reach more and more players. Everyday we grow bigger and bigger, and because of that we are grateful for every admin and developer who put their effort into the server to make it a better place.
