Requirements

System Requirements (minimal)

  • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E6600 / AMD Athlon 64 X2 4800+ or above
  • Memory: 6GB
  • Grapics: NVIDIA GeForce 8800 GT / ATI Radeon HD 4850 or above
  • DirectX version: 10 or above
  • Network: Internet connection required

System Requirements (highly recommended)

  • Processor: Intel Core i5-2400 / AMD FX-6300 or above
  • Memory: 8GB
  • Grapics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 750 Ti / AMD Radeon R7 260X
  • DirectX version: 10 or above
  • Network: Stable internet connection required

Application Requirements

  • Steam (For the game "Brick Rigs")
  • Discord (Optional but recommended)
