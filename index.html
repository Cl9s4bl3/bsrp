There's more to it, than you might think

Bricksville State Roleplay is one of the most realistic roleplaying server in Brick Rigs. Created in 2022, the server has been able to reach and change way of roleplaying in Brick Rigs for hundreds of people. Here at BSRP we try to provide our users with the best experience that they have ever had and try to focus on users' requests.

It is important to note, that the overall experience may vary on your system and the game.
To learn more, check out our features and our Discord server.
